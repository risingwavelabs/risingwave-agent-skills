- perf- | Performance & Memory | CRITICAL
- storage- | Storage & Compaction | CRITICAL
- source- | Sources | HIGH
- sink- | Sinks | HIGH
- cdc- | CDC | HIGH
- cluster- | Cluster Operations | HIGH
- ops- | Operational Best Practices | CRITICAL
- diag- | Diagnostic Queries | HIGH
- monitor- | Monitoring & Alerts | MEDIUM
